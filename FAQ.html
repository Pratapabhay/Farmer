1. How do I avail the services of this software?

Register yourself with a unique username and you are good to go. You can thereafter choose as many vegetables as you want from the menu provided and it will be delivered at your doorstep within 24 hours.


2. After how much time will my order be delivered?

It will be delivered the next day of your order.


3. Can I change my order if I want?

Yes, you can modify your order (add/remove veggies) but only untill that day itself. The changes made to your order henceforth will not be assuredly reflected in your delivered order.


4. What if the vegetables I want are not in the menu?

Then you obviously have to wait for the next day's menu to come (the menu updates daily) in which your required vegetable might be available.
